Where Engineers
Become Founders.

The Engineering Innovators and Entrepreneurs Club (EIEC) is a student-run organization at UC San Diego that turns engineering talent into entrepreneurial builders. We work with real ventures and give students the experience of building from zero to one.

Our Mission

Turning engineers into innovators and entrepreneurs.

At EIEC, we empower UC San Diego students to build ventures, solve real-world problems, and create what doesn't yet exist. We're shifting the campus focus from purely job-oriented pursuits toward innovation and entrepreneurship.

We foster an environment where students develop entrepreneurial thinking, tackle real challenges, and merge technical skills with business acumen. Our members don't just gain professional skills. They build the mindset and network to imagine, lead, and create.
